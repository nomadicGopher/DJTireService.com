Who we are

Is it about time your vehicle gets a new set of tires, a checkup or other repairs? Let us bring ASE certified services and mobile tire installation to you with our top of the line trailer of equipment. At DJ Tire, we understand how busy life gets, and it can sometimes be difficult finding the time to get your vehicle serviced and repaired. Whether you need assistance shopping for tires, bought your own and need them installed, or need an estimate or diagnosis on any other issue your vehicle is experiencing, we have your back!

Our expert team is able to service your vehicle by way of mobile service within 40 miles of our home base in Geneva Ohio. Drop your vehicle off to us, or we also offer pick up and drop off services within 15 miles. As always, you get flexible evenings and weekend service hours to further accommodate your busy lifestyle.

Services we provide


Wheels

As our primary service, we ensure that the wheels on your vehicles are balanced and equipped with quality tires and valve stems or Tire Pressure Monitoring Systems (TPMS) to keep you safe on the road.

In the special circumstance that your wheel(s) aren't currently on your vehicle, not a problem!

Steering and Suspension

Don't forget to have us check your alignment to ensure that you get the most life out of your tires and not end up with uneven tire wear.

We can also take care of your steering and suspension components!

General Maintenance

Keeping your vehicle well maintained is crucial to having a longer lifespan. We can take care of the basics such as brakes, filters, fluids, lights and batteries.

If there is something else that you are unsure if we cover, call or email us and we can let you know.
